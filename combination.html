Combination skin

You have combination skin, which means you have an oily t-zone and dry cheeks. No need to worry we've also got a routine for this issue. Since you have both dry and oily skin it's helpful to also check out our oily and dry skin section.
Here's some amazing ingredients for your skin:

Hyaluronic acid

It is naturally found in our skin. When applying it directly on our skin it helps to hydrate it. It gives you a plump look. It especially amazing for you dry cheeks to help them retain that moisture.

Salicylic acid

It is a beta hydroxy acid. Salicylic acid unclogs pores, fights against blemishes and exfoliates. Concentrate this ingredient on your oily spots to regulate the production of oil.
Here’s some ingredients you should not mix with salicylic acid:

Retinol
Glycolic Acid
Vitamin C

Here’s a simple skin care routine you can follow for your combination skin:

  1. Cleanse your skin gently. (AM and PM)

    2. These cleansers get rid of your oil build-up, without drying your skin out.

  2. Hydrate (AM and PM)

    3. Hyaluronic acid has lots of benefits and is a main component in our skin structure. It's very moisturizing and has anti-aging effects. The express "acid" might scare a lot of people off, but this ingredient doesn't cause allergic reactions because our bodies produce it. It's great to apply it before your other serums because it helps to seal in moisture. Apply it generously on your dry skin patches.

  3. Treat (AM and/or PM)

    4. Both niacinamide help to regulate oil-production. Apply either one of these ingredients onto your oily skin patches.

  4. Moisturize (AM and PM)

    5. Combination requires two different types of moisturizers, which can be annoying but is the most helpful. For your oily zones use a light-weight water-based formula. For the dry skin zones use a creamy moisturizer like the lipikar baume from la roche-posay.

  5. SPF (AM)

    6. For your SPF we chose a sunscreen that controls shine. Since the centre of your face gets quite oily it can be difficult to find a good spf.
