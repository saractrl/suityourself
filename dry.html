Dry skin

You have dry skin... this section will help you to relieve the feeling of itchiness and dryness in your skin. Here's some great ingredients for dehydrated skin:

Vitamin C

It is an antioxidant. But what is an antioxidant?
They're molecules that fight free radicals in our body, but why do they need to be fought? Simply said, if there's too many free radicals in our body they can cause harm. Especially fruits are filled with these so called antioxidants.
Not only can vitamin C be ingested, furthermore it can used on the skin to aid in water retention (= the ability to hold moisture/water)

Hyaluronic acid

It is naturally found in our skin. When applying it directly on our skin it helps to hydrate it. It gives you a plump look.
