Introduction

Welcome to the skin section of our page.

In this section we’ll focus on how to treat the skin according to the different skin types that exist.
Normally we speak about dry, combination and oily skin. On this page we also added the section called “acne-prone”, which is the most common skin concern.
If you experience any other serious skin disorders, we advise you to consult with a professional dermatologist or your doctor.
Please remember that everybody's skin is different, and things might not work for your skin. The skin is the largest organ of your body and reflects your physical, mental well-being and gut health. So in addition to these products, we recommend you to upkeep a healthy lifestyle and habits.

Now moving on, how do you determine your skin type?
We have curated some simple steps for you to find out:

  1. Gently cleanse your face with a cleanser.
  2. Pat it dry.
  3. Do not use any additional products on your face.
  4. After 30 minutes examine your face for any shine, what do you see?

Results:

If your skin feels tight → You have dry skin.
If you have shine on nose and forehead → You have combination skin.
If you shine on your cheeks in addition to nose and forehead → You have oily skin.
You can also use a thin tissue to pat your skin to see how much and where you produced oil.
Now you can click on the links to find skin care for your specific skin type!

How to perform a patch test before trying new cosmetics:

  1. Choose an area of your skin.
  2. Apply the product
  3. Leave the are alone for up to 48 hours

If you don't experience any difficulties, you can use the product. Although it's safe to use, we recommend to use it in moderation at first.
